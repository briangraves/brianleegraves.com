About

I'm a Kansas City-based technology leader focused on the use of future-friendly processes to build digital products and services. I'm a strong proponent of iterative work, collaborative efforts, integrative thinking, and user first design.

Currently, I'm Vice President of Engineering at Forum One where I lead our Technical Services department in the process of efficiently delivering innovative, reliable, and purpose-driven technology for mission-driven clients through cutting edge development practices, rapid and cumulative learning, and collaboration within the organization.

When not immersed in technology, I can be found around Kansas City trying out new dinner spots, following Kansas basketball and Chiefs footbal, or at home discovering new music, working on my cooking skills, and hanging out with my wife, two kids and our miniature Australian Shepherds.
